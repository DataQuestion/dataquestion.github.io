Welcome to "Data Question"! This blog aims to document my personal journey into the world of data analysis. Here, you'll find my step-by-step progress as I dive into hands-on projects exploring real-world data. In every project, I start with a main question and the entire process revolves around answering that question. Along the way, additional questions naturally arise as I explore deeper, each one driving me closer to a conclusion. I am committed to sharing what I learn with you whenever I gain new knowledge.

Whether you're a fellow beginner looking to learn alongside me or someone curious about the process of data analysis, this blog aims to provide a transparent and comprehensive look at the skills, tools, and concepts that form the foundation of the field. If you are a professional programmer, please feel free to advise me (when I add a comment section on my website). I welcome your guidance and feedback as I strive to improve. Join me as I explore everything from the foundational concepts to more advanced projects, demystifying the data that shapes our world.
