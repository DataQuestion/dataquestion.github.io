What is the definition of population?

The term population means (1) all the inhabitants of a given country or area (province, city, metropolitan area etc.) considered together; the number of inhabitants of a country of area. (2) In sampling, the whole collection of units (persons, households, institutions, events etc.) from which a sample may be drawn.

reference: United Nations, Department of Economic and Social Affairs, Population Division (2024). World Population Prospects 2024: Methodology of the United Nations population estimates and projections. UN DESA/POP/2024/DC/NO. 10, July 2024 [Advance unedited version].
